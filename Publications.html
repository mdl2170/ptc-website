Books, essays, and field notes on conflict.

Latest · 2nd Edition · 2026

The Way Out: How to Overcome Toxic Polarization.

Drawing on three decades of conflict research, Coleman maps a path through the deepest political fractures of our time. The new edition adds a preface for the AI era and three chapters on how machine-mediated discourse is reshaping the way we disagree — and what to do about it.

Columbia University Press · Hardcover, paperback, audiobook · Foreword by Maria Ressa
